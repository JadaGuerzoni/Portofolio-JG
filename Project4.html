Automatic construction progress monitoring FPT-AI

A computer vision application that analyzes UAV footage and compares it with a 3D structural model to automatically monitor construction progress.

Year
2026
Type
Industry Project
Stack
AI - Mast3r-slamPointcloudUnityC#BIM modelsHTMLCSSJavaScript

Overview

FPT-AI is a progress-monitoring concept that connects aerial video analysis with a 3D model so construction status can be checked more automatically.

Pipeline

  • UAV footage is processed through a computer-vision pipeline
  • Point cloud data is compared against the BIM / structural model
  • Mast3r-slam and related AI tooling support the alignment and analysis
  • Unity and C# are used to present or integrate the result visually

Why it matters

The project is about reducing manual progress checking and making site comparison more consistent, especially when construction teams need a quick overview of what has changed.
